GYM PROGRESSION GUIDE — HYPERTROPHY & STRENGTH

CORE PRINCIPLES
- Progressive overload: add weight or reps every session/week
- Hypertrophy rep range: 8–12 reps per set
- Strength rep range: 3–6 reps per set
- Rest between sets: 90–120 seconds for hypertrophy, 2–3 min for strength
- Add 2.5kg when you can complete all sets at the top of the rep range

WEEKLY PROGRESSION MODEL (DOUBLE PROGRESSION)
- Set a rep range e.g. 3 sets of 8–12
- Stay at same weight until you hit 12 reps on ALL sets
- Only then increase weight by 2.5–5kg and drop back to 8 reps
- This guarantees consistent, trackable progress every week

---

CHEST

BENCH PRESS
- Muscles: chest, front deltoids, triceps
- Beginner: 3x10 — add 2.5kg each week
- Intermediate: 4x8 working sets — add 2.5kg every 1–2 weeks
- Progression tip: if stuck, do pause reps at the bottom or add a drop set

INCLINE BENCH PRESS
- Muscles: upper chest, front deltoids, triceps
- Angle: 30–45 degrees — higher angle shifts more work to shoulders
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 8–12
- Add 2.5kg when all sets hit 12 reps
- Tip: upper chest is often a weak point, prioritize this early in your session

MACHINE PEC DECK (FLY)
- Muscles: chest (inner and outer), front delts
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 12–15
- Squeeze hard at the peak contraction for 1 second
- Great as a finisher after pressing movements
- Add weight when all sets hit 15 reps cleanly

WEIGHTED DIPS
- Muscles: lower chest, triceps, front deltoids
- Beginner: bodyweight 3x8 before adding weight
- Intermediate: attach plate via dip belt, 3–4 sets of 8–10
- Lean forward slightly to bias chest, stay upright to bias triceps
- Add 2.5kg when all sets hit 10 reps

---

BACK

LAT PULLDOWN
- Muscles: lats, biceps, rear delts
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 10–12
- Grip variations: wide overhand (width), close neutral (thickness)
- Add 5kg when you hit 12 reps on all sets cleanly

CABLE ROWS
- Muscles: lats, rhomboids, rear delts, biceps
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 10–12
- Drive elbows back, squeeze at the end of each rep

CHEST SUPPORTED T-BAR ROW
- Muscles: mid back, rhomboids, rear delts, biceps
- Chest supported means no lower back involvement — pure back work
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 10–12
- Drive elbows toward the ceiling, hold contraction 1 second at top
- Add weight when all sets hit 12 reps with full range of motion

PULL UP
- Muscles: lats, biceps, rear delts, core
- Beginner: use assisted machine or band — aim for 3x5 to start
- Intermediate: 3–4 sets of 8–12 bodyweight
- Advanced: add weight via belt, same double progression model
- Tip: dead hang at the bottom, chin fully over bar at the top

DEADLIFT
- Muscles: hamstrings, glutes, lower back, traps, forearms
- Beginner: 3x5 — add 5kg each week
- Intermediate: 3x4–6 — add 2.5kg every 1–2 weeks
- Always prioritize form over weight — hinge at hips, neutral spine

ROMANIAN DEADLIFT (RDL)
- Muscles: hamstrings, glutes, lower back (hip hinge pattern)
- Different from deadlift: knees stay slightly bent, bar stays close to legs
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 8–12
- Feel a deep stretch in the hamstrings at the bottom
- Add 2.5kg when all sets hit 12 reps with full stretch

---

SHOULDERS

OVERHEAD PRESS (OHP)
- Muscles: shoulders, triceps, upper chest
- Rep range: 4x6–10
- Slowest lift to progress — add 1.25kg when stuck
- Tip: brace core hard, do not hyperextend lower back

LATERAL RAISE
- Muscles: lateral deltoid (shoulder width)
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 15–20
- Use light weight — form breaks very easily on this exercise
- Lead with elbows not hands, stop at shoulder height
- Add 1–2kg only when all sets hit 20 reps cleanly

REVERSE PEC DECK
- Muscles: rear deltoids, rhomboids, mid traps
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 12–15
- Most people neglect rear delts — this directly fixes shoulder imbalance
- Keep a slight bend in elbows, squeeze shoulder blades together at peak

DUMBBELL SHRUGS
- Muscles: upper traps, neck
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 12–15
- Hold the shrug at the top for 1–2 seconds, do not roll shoulders
- Add 2.5kg when all sets hit 15 reps

---

ARMS

BICEP CURLS
- Muscles: biceps brachii, brachialis
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 10–15
- Variations: barbell curl, dumbbell curl, hammer curl, cable curl
- Add 2.5kg when all sets reach 15 reps

BAYESIAN CABLE CURL
- Muscles: biceps (long head emphasis, peak contraction)
- Setup: low cable behind you, curl forward while leaning slightly forward
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 12–15
- Superior stretch on the bicep compared to standard curls
- Add weight when all sets hit 15 reps

PREACHER CURL
- Muscles: biceps (short head, lower bicep thickness)
- Arm rests on pad — eliminates cheating and shoulder involvement
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 10–12
- Add 2.5kg when all sets hit 12 reps with full range of motion

TRICEP EXTENSION
- Muscles: triceps (long head focus)
- Variations: cable pushdown, overhead extension, skull crushers
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 12–15
- Add weight when all sets hit 15 reps with clean form

OVERHEAD CABLE TRICEP EXTENSION
- Muscles: triceps long head (best stretch of all tricep exercises)
- Setup: high cable, face away, extend arms overhead
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 12–15
- Long head only fully stretched in overhead position — this is key
- Add weight when all sets hit 15 reps

DUMBBELL WRIST CURLS AND EXTENSIONS
- Muscles: forearm flexors (curls) and forearm extensors (extensions)
- Rep range: 3 sets of 15–20 each direction
- Use light dumbbells, rest forearm on knee or bench
- Often neglected — directly improves grip strength for deadlifts and rows

---

LEGS

SQUAT
- Muscles: quads, glutes, hamstrings, core
- Beginner: 3x5 goblet squat to learn form, then move to barbell
- Intermediate: 4x6–8 barbell back squat — add 2.5–5kg each week
- Depth: aim for parallel or below
- Tip: knees track over toes, chest stays up, brace core before every rep

ROMANIAN DEADLIFT (RDL)
- See Back section above — also a primary hamstring exercise

SEATED LEG CURL
- Muscles: hamstrings (short head, knee flexion)
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 10–15
- Pause at peak contraction, control the negative
- Add weight when all sets hit 15 reps

LEG EXTENSION
- Muscles: quadriceps (isolation)
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 12–15
- Straighten fully at the top, hold 1 second
- Best used as a finisher after squats

WALKING LUNGE
- Muscles: quads, glutes, hamstrings, balance
- Rep range: 3 sets of 10–12 reps per leg
- Add dumbbells once bodyweight becomes easy
- Keep torso upright, front knee does not go past toes

NAUTILUS GLUTE DRIVE (HIP THRUST MACHINE)
- Muscles: glutes (primary), hamstrings (secondary)
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 10–15
- Squeeze glutes hard at the top, hold 2 seconds
- One of the best direct glute exercises available
- Add weight when all sets hit 15 reps

STANDING CALF RAISE
- Muscles: gastrocnemius (upper calf)
- Rep range: 4 sets of 15–20
- Full range of motion: deep stretch at bottom, full extension at top
- Calves are stubborn — train them with high reps and slow tempo

---

CORE

CABLE CRUNCH
- Muscles: rectus abdominis (upper and lower abs)
- Rep range: 3–4 sets of 12–15
- Kneel facing the cable, crunch down with elbows toward knees
- Resist the urge to use hip flexors — initiate the movement from abs
- Add weight when all sets hit 15 reps cleanly

---

NECK

NECK CURLS AND EXTENSIONS
- Muscles: neck flexors (curls) and neck extensors (extensions)
- Rep range: 3 sets of 15–20 each direction
- Use a neck harness or plate held on forehead/back of head
- Start very light — neck muscles are easy to strain
- Builds a thick athletic neck, also reduces injury risk

SAMPLE PUSH / PULL / LEGS SPLIT

PUSH DAY (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
- Bench press 4x8
- Incline bench press 3x10
- Overhead press 3x8
- Lateral raise 3x15
- Machine pec deck 3x12
- Weighted dips 3x10
- Overhead cable tricep extension 3x12

PULL DAY (Back, Biceps, Rear Delts)
- Deadlift 3x5
- Pull up 3x8
- Chest supported T-bar row 4x10
- Lat pulldown 3x12
- Reverse pec deck 3x15
- Bayesian cable curl 3x12
- Preacher curl 3x10
- Dumbbell shrugs 3x12

LEGS DAY (Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves)
- Squat 4x6
- Romanian deadlift 3x10
- Leg extension 3x12
- Seated leg curl 3x12
- Walking lunge 3x10 each leg
- Nautilus glute drive 3x12
- Standing calf raise 4x15

ACCESSORY (can be added to any day)
- Cable crunch 3x15
- Neck curls and extensions 3x15
- Dumbbell wrist curls and extensions 3x15
